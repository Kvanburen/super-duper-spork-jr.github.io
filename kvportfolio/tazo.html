Tazo Tea Ad

This is a series of ads I created for Tazo tea. There are three ads in total, each designed to feel cohesive with the rest of the series and use the same assets while keeping their individuality.

The product photos of the tea packets were taken by myself then masked and color corrected to be used within the ads. To show off the naturalness of the product I added in a Camellia sinensis which is the plant green teas originate from, as well as some dried tea leaves. The last asset I needed was a catchy slogan to keep the viewers attention that was quick and memorable so I used the brands slogan “It begins with tea.”

Once I had the backbone assets that would be placed in each ad assembled together I dived into adding varying colors, shapes, and text formatting to make the ads fun, bright, and eye catching.
